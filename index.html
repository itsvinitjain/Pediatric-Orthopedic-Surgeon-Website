About Us

Dr. Deepak Jain is a consultant paediatric orthopedic surgeon practicing in Navi Mumbai.
He specializes in treating various paediatric orthopedic disorders like paediatric trauma, Knock knees, Bowlegs, Deformity correction, Limb lengthening, CTEV, Congenital Vertical talus, pseudoarthrosis tibia fibula, cerebral palsy, septic arthritis, Osteomyelitis, benign tumors.
He has a special interest in deformity correction and hip Preservation surgeries.
